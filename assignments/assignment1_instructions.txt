NOTE: Q4 may appear as "Identify the critical issues using bullet points..."
in some student submissions. These are the SAME question — do not grade it twice.
The student only needs to answer this once in any format.

CRITICAL — TOPIC OF THIS ASSIGNMENT:
All questions are specifically about the SPECIALIST REFERRAL process at VWC —
i.e., when a primary care physician refers a patient to see a specialist.
The referral workflow involves: physician determines need for specialist,
insurance/prior authorization, coordinating with specialist office, sending
clinical records, patient follow-up after specialist visit, closing the loop.

A student who writes about PATIENT SCHEDULING (appointment booking, slot
matching, check-in/check-out, no-show management) instead of SPECIALIST
REFERRALS has answered a DIFFERENT question and should be marked on_topic=false.
