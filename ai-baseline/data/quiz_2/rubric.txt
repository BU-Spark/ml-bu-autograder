
Question (15 points)

Describe how one function of revenue cycle management system functionality increases a provider’s revenue.
Short answer of two to four sentences in your own words. Cite any sources you use including GenAI.

Grading Rubric:
• Up to 4 points for identifying a reasonable function
• Up to 11 points for explaining how it increases revenue
• Maximum: 15 points

Possible functions (others are acceptable):

Automated Eligibility Verification: Confirms insurance coverage before services, reducing denials and increasing successful reimbursement.
Claims Scrubbing: Detects errors before submission to reduce denials and rework and speed reimbursement.
Denial Management: Analyzes denial patterns and assists with appeals to recover lost revenue.
Charge Capture Automation: Ensures all services are recorded and billed accurately.
Contract Management: Compares payments to payer contracts to identify underpayments.
Prior Authorization Tools: Automates authorizations to prevent delays and denials.
Patient Payment Estimation: Provides cost estimates to improve patient collections.
Automated Coding Assistance: Helps assign accurate codes to reduce billing errors and maximize reimbursement.
Automated Coding Audits: Ensures compliance and reduces revenue loss from penalties.
Self-Pay and Payment Plan Management: Improves patient balance collection.
Real-Time Analytics and Reporting: Identifies financial performance issues to optimize billing.
Claim Status Tracking: Monitors claims to ensure timely payments and prevent lost claims.
Patient Portals: Allows online bill viewing and payment to improve collections.
Predictive Analytics for Payer Behavior: Anticipates reimbursement issues and improves strategy.
